AVENGERS: END GAME

Directed By: Anthony Russo, Joe Russo

Written By: Christopher Markus, Stephen McFeely

Run Time: 181 min

Release Date: April 26, 2019


After the devastating events of Avengers:Infinity War (2018),the universe is in ruins.
With the help of remaining allies, the Avengers assemble once more in order to undo Thanos' actions and restore order to the universe.

This movie is like a final season from a sequence of 21 titles, so to have total experience in this movie you should have watched at least a half of this 21 titles and of course you MUST have watched AVENGERS: INFINITY WAR because "END GAME" is the directly sequence of it.


This is a movie with 3 hours of a mix of feelings where you will be sometimes smiling, sad, hopeful and with all together. This time the Avengers are very upset about what happened in Infinity war, they are trying to find someway to reverse what Thanos did, but this will not be easy since the team it's broken and someone died.
